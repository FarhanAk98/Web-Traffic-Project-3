The Click and Grow smart garden works by providing plants with everything they need for optimal growth: water, nutrients, light, and airflow. Here’s a step-by-step breakdown of how it works:

  1. Insert Pre-Seeded Pods: Start by inserting your pre-seeded plant pods into the garden. Each pod contains a seed, soil, and nutrients specific to the type of plant. Choose from a variety of plants available on the EDN Tech website, including herbs, vegetables, and flowers.
  2. Add Water: Fill the water reservoir of the Click and Grow smart garden. The system will take care of the rest, ensuring that your plants receive the right amount of water at the right time.
  3. Turn on the Grow Lights: The system comes with integrated grow lights for indoor plants, which will automatically turn on and adjust based on the light needs of your plants. These energy-efficient LED lights mimic natural sunlight, providing your plants with the perfect spectrum of light for photosynthesis.
  4. Monitor and Enjoy: Using the app, you can monitor the progress of your plants, receive notifications when it's time to add more water, and track their growth. Simply sit back and watch your plants grow!
